## Main Profile Overview

I build AI agents and automation systems for business workflows.

I help teams turn repeatable manual work into reliable AI-assisted systems:
research agents, reporting agents, CRM and sales ops copilots, support
assistants, ecommerce workflows, knowledge-base/RAG tools, API integrations,
dashboards, alerts, and internal operations tools.

A good AI agent is not just a chatbot. It needs clear inputs, connected tools,
business rules, memory or knowledge retrieval when useful, logging, review
points, and a predictable output format. I focus on shipping systems that fit
the way your team already works.

Typical builds include:

- OpenAI, Claude, and LLM API integrations
- RAG and knowledge-base search over documents, websites, or internal data
- Research agents that collect, summarize, enrich, and structure information
- CRM and sales operations copilots
- Customer support and internal operations assistants
- AI-assisted reporting, alerts, and dashboards
- n8n, Zapier, Make, Airtable, Google Sheets, Slack, email, and API workflows
- Custom internal tools around repeatable business processes

I also have deep experience with trading software, broker APIs, market data,
dashboards, backtesting, and execution tooling. I use that background as proof
that I can handle complex APIs and data-heavy systems. I do not sell AI as a
guaranteed way to generate trading returns.

If you have a workflow that is currently manual, repetitive, or spread across
too many tools, I can help turn it into a clear AI-assisted system with defined
inputs, outputs, review steps, and success criteria.
